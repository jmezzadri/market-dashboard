06 Risk Management

What could break this view.

The four scenarios that would flip the model's recommendation. Each has a Trigger (the macro condition that would fire it) and an Alternate (where the portfolio would move).

Crude breaks below $70/bbl

Energy goes from cash machine to capital trap. Cash flow turns vulnerable; capital discipline narrative collapses.

Alternate: rotate Energy back to Market Weight, add weight to Health Care and Staples for defensive carry.

Credit spreads widen > 100 bp in two weeks

Risk & Liquidity composite would flip from calm to elevated. Aggressive stance no longer justified; cyclicals at risk of repricing.

Alternate: trim Capital Markets and Software, raise defensive ballast from 14% to 30%, shift stance to Balanced.

Unemployment claims spike above 280k for three weeks

Growth composite rolls over fast. Consumer Discretionary and Capital Markets repriced first; AI capex demand follows with a lag.

Alternate: shift to Defensive stance. Overweights collapse to Health Care + Staples; equity exposure cut to 60%.

10-year yield breaks above 5.0%

Inflation & Rates composite turns stressed. Long-duration cyclicals (Real Estate, Utilities, Software) repriced lower.

Alternate: cut Software to Market Weight, hold Energy and Banks (rate beneficiaries), defensive ballast to 25%.
